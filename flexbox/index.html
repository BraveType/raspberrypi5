About

Hi i am leonhard Katschner and I run Brave Type.

I have an education as digital media designer and I am a freelance type designer. I make my fonts with a lot of passion and I try to give every typeface its own voice & character and put my own style in it.

Brave is the key word here as I am producing fonts that want to be brave and look different than what exists already.

I was born in berlin and I still live in Berlin since 1988. It is a fabulous city and I can recommend it to everyone. Here I live in a cosy flat with my dear wife and my amazing daughter.

In my freetime I like to visualize my fonts with Blender, Photoshop and Animate. Also I walk around Berlin with my little family, discovering new sites, lakes & playing grounds.
